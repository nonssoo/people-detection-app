Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the detection?

Our system maintains over 95% accuracy under optimal lighting and positioning using YOLOv8 and pose tracking models.

Does it require internet?

The detection runs locally on edge devices. Internet is only required for dashboard sync and cloud backups.

Can it differentiate between staff and visitors?

Yes. With labeled training and badge detection, the system can classify user roles in real time.
